How This Works

This app runs fully in your browser. No backend is required.

  1. Drop a PDF or TXT file anywhere on the screen.
  2. The app extracts text and tokenizes it.
  3. In Basic mode, type one question. In Advanced mode, create multiple filter boxes and enable the ones you want.
  4. Matching tokens between query/filter terms and the document are color-linked.
  5. The Top Pages section ranks pages by how many linked token hits they contain.
  6. Click a ranked page to jump directly to it in the document panel.

Tip: click linked tags to turn them on/off for matching and ranking. Hover still previews highlights.
